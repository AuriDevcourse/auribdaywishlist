## How to Use the Wishlist

### How do I see what gifts are available?
Scroll down to see all the gift cards. Each card starts face-down - tap on it to reveal the gift!

### How do I reserve a gift?
1. Tap on a card to reveal the gift
2. Wait for the buttons to appear (about 1.5 seconds)
3. Click "Reserve" button
4. The gift will be marked as reserved

### How do I buy a gift?
1. Tap on a card to reveal the gift
2. Click the "Link" button
3. You'll be taken to the store where you can purchase the gift

### Can I unreserve a gift?
Yes! If you reserved a gift but changed your mind, click the "Unreserve" button that appears on reserved items.

### What does "Reserved" mean?
When a gift shows "Reserved", it means someone has already claimed it. Please choose a different gift to avoid duplicates!

### Can I see who reserved a gift?

Clicking "Reveal All" will flip all the cards at once so you can see all the gifts without tapping each one individually.

## Technical Issues

### The cards won't flip
Try refreshing the page. Make sure JavaScript is enabled in your browser.

### I can't reserve a gift
Make sure you've revealed the card first by tapping on it, then wait for the buttons to appear.

### The page looks broken
Try clearing your browser cache and refreshing the page. The site works best on modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari.
